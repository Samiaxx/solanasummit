Creative direction

Nigerian heritage, Solana speed, global streetwear.

The concept avoids costume references. It abstracts Nigerian textile systems into modern apparel graphics that feel premium, culturally relevant, and wearable beyond the event.

Cultural InspirationAdire-inspired resist lines become circuit paths, linking Nigerian textile memory to Internet Capital Markets.
Nigerian HeritageIgbo uli curves, northern geometry, woven bands, and Akwa Ibom resort culture influence patterns, trims, and silhouettes.
Solana AlignmentSummit lime, Solana mint, deep green, and indigo create a future-facing visual system connected to web3 liquidity and speed.
Typography + SymbolismCondensed uppercase type creates a poster-like summit voice; brass marks value and craft; lime bands signal market energy.
